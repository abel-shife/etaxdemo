Welcome to the E-Tax System!

E-Tax for SIGTAS is your modern, secure, and user-friendly platform for managing tax declarations, accounts, and services. Whether you are a taxpayer or a tax officer, this guide will help you get started.

Getting Started

  1. Read this onboarding guide to understand the main features.
  2. Login as a Taxpayer or Tax Officer using your credentials.
  3. Explore your dashboard, manage declarations, view reports, and use the message centre.
  4. Access help and support anytime from the navigation bar.
